Our Drinks

We specialized in a fruity beer. We wanted to create a beer that was easy to drink for everyone whilst also maintaining a normal achohol percentage. Our beer has a percentage of 5% We believe that this, combined with a fruity refreshing taste is the key to a good beer. The fruity taste also makes is easier to drink during a power hours.
